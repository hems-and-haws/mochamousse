Our Story

When I was a young lass, I would often spend summers with my grandparents in Cape Spear, Newfoundland.

There was a remarkably cozy cafe just down the street from their cottage, and on the dreary days, I would stop in for a quick pastry and a hot cup of tea, while I caught up on my summer reading assignments.

I loved that cafe! While the menu was simple, they always had the most delicious fresh baked bread. Everyone in town knew eachother, and townsfolk would often stop in to catch up on the latest news or gossip.

One day I thought to myself "I wish I could take that cozy cafe and transport it back to Tampa, FL when I return home!"

...And that's how the idea for Mocha Mousse was born!

-Owner, Marigold Decimals
