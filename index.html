Welcome to Mocha Mousse Cafe!

We're a small local business based in Tampa, FL. We sell coffee, tea, pastries, and some of the yummiest sandwiches in all of Florida. If you haven't stopped by in a while, we hope you will soon! We're confident that once you taste our fresh baked bread, or sip one of our many specialty or seasonal beverages, you'll make a stop our shop part of your regular routine.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a calm and welcoming community gathering space where our customers can treat themselves to simple indulgences like healthy, quick lunch featuring our fresh bread - baked in-house daily, or our gather and connect with friends and family over a cup of our high-quality, sustainably sourced coffee.

Commitment to quality

When you step into one of our cafes, you can feel it instantly. The perfect getaway for work, play, or study complimented with great tasting coffee.
Mocha Mousse was built on an unwavering culture to provide a one-of-a-kind experience. Our focus is community. Our passion is coffee...and baked goods!
